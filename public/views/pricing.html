Frequently Asked Questions

How does the monthly vs yearly billing work?

Yearly billing gives you 2 months completely free! You pay for 10 months up front, and get access to your selected plan features for a full year.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade to a higher tier plan or downgrade to a lower tier directly from your dashboard anytime you like.

Are there any hidden setup fees or contact lock-ins?

No. Our plans are fully transparent, contract-free, and can be cancelled at any time without any penalties or fees.
